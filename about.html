About us


AzhaguNila was started on 28th July , 2021 as Trust foundation for Visually Challenged persons , who have no access to good stay , healthy food and quality education.

We at AzhaguNila takecare of those who are in need , through volunteers and the invisible helping hand that showers the world with love and care.

How we started.

Our founder-President of AzhaguNila, Mr. Karuppa Samy B.A., M.A., B.Ed., M.Ed., founded this trust to ensure good and safest path for the visually challenged persons to get thier basic needs i.e Food, Education and Job-Security
